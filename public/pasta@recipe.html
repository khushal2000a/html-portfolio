Pasta

Ingredients

  • Fusilli pasta
  • Pasta sauce
  • Pasta masala
  • General vegetables
  • Parmesan regiano

Recipe

  1. Boil pasta, keep it aside and massage with oil
  2. Now in pan saute vegetables in oil and add salt and spices
  3. Add pasta and some water to make gravy consistent
  4. Add pasta and let it cook for 5 mins
  5. Serve hot and saucy pasta to bowl and garnise with Parmesan regiano and coriander
